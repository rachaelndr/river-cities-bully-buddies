Who We Are

Founded in 2012 as private animal rescue, RCBB officially became a nonprofit in 2015 and is the longest standing registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit in the immediate tri-state area of WV, KY, OH dedicated to the rescue of bully breed dogs who are at urgent risk in local shelters.

We are a small 100% volunteer run, foster home based organization located in Huntington, WV, and immediate surrounding tri-state area of WV, KY, OH. We are dedicated to the rescue of bully breed dogs who are at high risk of euthanasia in local shelters. We spay/neuter, provide medical care, food and all other necessities. Each dog in our care is loved as a family member by our foster families and integrated into our daily lives where we get to know their personalities to match them with the best possible forever home.

In addition to our rescue and re-homing efforts, we strive to increase positive public awareness, protect dog’s labeled “pit bull” from discrimination, and the importance of treating each dog as an individual, regardless of breed or physical appearance. RCBB also provides assistance to help reduce the number of dogs being surrendered to shelters and rescues through our spay/neuter assistance programs and helping families facing breed discrimination issues. RCBB promotes compassion and responsibility in our community towards all animals.

Dogs we take into the program include, but not limited to:

  • Dogs labeled "pit bull"
  • English Bull Terriers
  • Boxers
  • American/Alapaha/English/Oldie English/French Bulldogs
  • Cane Corso
  • Presa Canario Mastiffs
  • and mixes there of...
