Adoption Policy

Our goal is to make the best possible match! If your application is approved a volunteer will contact you to set up a home-based meet and greet with the dog you are interested in. If all goes well, we will then set up a home visit and ask that a two week trial period be given to determine if the dog is a good fit for your family. RCBB does not want anyone to feel pressured to keep a dog they don't have a good connection with. (The adoption fee minus $25 will be reimbursed during this 2-week trial period.) If you decided that the dog is a good fit, a contract will then be signed and finalized. (The process time can vary depending on volunteer availability)

  • Applicant must have non-discriminatory home owners insurance and/or have approval from landlord if renting.
  • We adopt to indoor homes only.
  • You must be at least 21 years of age to adopt.
  • Dogs already in the home must be spayed/neutered and up to date on vaccines.
  • Our "pit bull" dogs: We DO NOT adopt out in areas with Breed Discriminatory laws, including within the city limits of Huntington (restrictions) or Barboursville (restrictions), Ceredo (ban), Bluefield (ban), Dunbar (restrictions), Montgomery (restrictions), Fayetteville (restrictions) WV....Rome Township in Proctorville, OH (ban), Flatwoods, KY (restrictions). NO EXCEPTIONS!
  • We require a home based meet and greet, so potential must be within reasonable driving distance of Huntington/Charleston, WV.
  • Dogs require a 12-15+ year commitment. Serious inquires only!

Adoption Fees

Our costs include vaccinations, spay/neuter, de-worming, heart worm test for dogs over 6 months, flea & heart worm preventative, microchip with free lifetime registration and often additional medical or surgical procedures for dogs who have been abused/neglected.

  • Puppies under 1 year of age: $175
  • Adults over 1 year of age: $150
  • Dogs 7 years and older: $100
  • Special circumstances: $200
