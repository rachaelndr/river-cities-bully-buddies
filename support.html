Sponsor a Rescue Dog

To help us in our efforts, we have decided to start a "Sponsor a Rescue Dog Program" to help us ensure that each dog in our care is happy and healthy while searching for their forever home! It is an excellent way you can help save a life, even if you are unable to foster or adopt. By sponsoring one of our dogs, you help provide him/her with food, toys, treats, and vet care! Sponsoring helps to offset our costs- in turn enabling us to help more dogs in need! Together we can make a difference!

RCBB will keep you informed of your sponsor dog’s progress. Your name will also appear on our page as a sponsor (in that dog’s album), or your sponsorship may remain anonymous if you prefer. Here is the list of sponsor levels, but of course any amount is accepted and appreciated.

Helping Paw- $10.00
Your donation helps provide toys and treats for a dog.
Home Again Hero- $20.00
Your donation will provide the most powerful tool to reunite a lost dog with their family- a microchip with a lifetime registration.
Chow Pal- $30.00
Your donation will purchase a bag of 4health or Diamond Naturals puppy/dog chow
Puppy Love- $50.00
Your donation will provide a pup with their first wellbeing checkup and all 3 distemper/parvo combination vaccines.
Fix-It- $65.00
The best way to reduce the number of shelter deaths: spay/neuter! Address the problem at its root and FIX IT! Your donation will spay/neuter a dog, and supply pain medicine for after surgery to keep him/her comfortable while healing.
Bully Buddy- $150.00+
Your donation will cover the cost to provide a dog with basic vet care. This includes: spay/neuter, vaccines (DHPPL and rabies), heartworm test, a dose of monthly flea/heartworm preventative and microchip with lifetime enrollment. Your name will be listed on the dog's bio as a "Bully Buddy" Sponsor. As an extra special thank you, you will receive a "Bully Buddy" t-shirt of your choice!
