Meet Asteria

Our beautiful lil' chocolate drop, Asteria is approximately 5 months old, on the small side and is a spunky and super affectionate gal! She is even prettier in person and has the most expressive eyes and face. She is a a sweet baby and loves playing with older kids and enjoys romping around with her furry foster friends (both dog and kitties). However, she can usually be found on her Foster Mom's heels as she seems to prefer human company over animal company. She can be shy/nervous upon first meetings and new things, however, it does not take her long to warm up and come out of her shell. She will need to be socialized with people and other animals, so a active on the go home with activities such as walking, going to the park, attending training classes, etc....is what we are looking for in a potential home so she can be the best girl she can be and not be afraid of the outside world. Despite her current minor set backs, she is a lovable little lady and so smart, eager to please!

Asteria's favorite things are snuggles, toys and romping around with her furry friends. She is doing great with house and crate training, but still has a way to go before it is mastered. This gal is very enthusiastic, eager to learn and highly treat motivated.

Puppies are a lot of work! If you do not have time, patience and some understanding, a puppy is not for you. Serious inquires only!
