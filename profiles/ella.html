Meet Ella

Ella is a lovely gal and absolutely stunning! 3 years old, weighing around 60lbs.

Good with Other Dogs? She has been fine with other dogs similar to her size and she LOVES (prefers) puppies! She has not shown much interest in playing with other adult dogs, but she has a blast with pups!

Cats? Ella does NOT get along with cats or other small animals.

Children? Ella has been great around all the people she has met, however, we are looking to place her in an adult only home or a home with older teens due to her size, strength & energy.

Activity Level: Ella needs to be in a active home with plenty of outdoor adventures and car rides! This girl is intelligent and picks up on routine quickly.

She is great company (Ella is independent, not a velcro dog, does her own thing) and has a fun personality. Someone is going to adopt a fantastic girl!
