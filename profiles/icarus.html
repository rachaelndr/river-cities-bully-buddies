Meet Icarus

Icarus is a one of a kind boy. He is sweet, loving, and playful little guy. Wonderful with dogs of all sizes and kids. Absolutely loves cuddles and play time with kids.

Icarus is still very much puppy, with that being said we are still working on potty training. Overall he does wonderful, with the exception of a few accidents.

Icarus knows sit, bed (his crate), dinner time, and door for potty time. Little man is wonderful on a leash. He loves walks around the neighborhood. Friendly with all animals he has come in contact with. We have a few stray cats in the area and he doesn't seem to mind or care that they are around.

Icarus is over all a perfect little guy. He has so much love to give and is all about his human.

Puppies are a lot of work! If you do not have time, patience and some understanding, a puppy is not for you. Serious inquires only!
